03 / The build

Every detail
handled.

Climacool® fabric

91/9 polyester-lycra flatlock. Two-way stretch. Breathes through a hot July gym, holds shape through a 6-tournament weekend.

True sublimation

Color is dyed into the fiber, not laid on top. No cracking, no peeling, no fade through 40+ wash cycles in a club season.

Volleyball-cut fit

Sleeve cap built for the swing. Side panel built for the dive. Hem built for the tuck. Liberos and outsides get the cut they actually need.

Names + numbers

Sublimated into the jersey, not stitched on or ironed on. Roster size, position, captain, libero — handled before the press runs.
